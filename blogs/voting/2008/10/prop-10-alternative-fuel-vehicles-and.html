Not Even the President Knows Enough to Vote

Sunday, October 26, 2008

Prop 10: Alternative Fuel Vehicles and Renewable Energy.

Let me start by saying that I don't use air conditioning, I don't water my lawn, I buy a tank of gas about once every 6 weeks, and despite NOT paying for my own electricity, I try to be careful.

Prop 10 is another complicated one. Is it REALLY going to help? Or is it a "trick" by money-hungry corporations trying to make money at any future expense?

I don't know. I hate not knowing. When I don't know, I generally vote NO.

AND, AND, I finally have a platform to say--why don't bicycle commuters get any tax credit when buying a bicycle for commuting? It's not fair. And I will take this chance to vote against a proposition which ignores bicycle commuters.

Vote NO on Prop 10.
